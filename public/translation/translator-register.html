SAYBON TRANSLATOR REGISTRATION

Join the SayBon Translator Pool and collaborate with us on professional document translation.

Who Can Apply

  • Must be proficient in French and English
  • Strong writing ability in both languages
  • Experienced translating professional documents
  • Ability to meet deadlines
  • Comfortable working inside the SayBon platform

Important Platform Rule

All translation work is completed strictly inside the SayBon platform.

  • Documents cannot be copied
  • Documents cannot be downloaded
  • Documents cannot be exported
  • All editing must happen inside the SayBon editor

Confidentiality and NDA

Before accessing any translation job, translators must sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA).

Because different clients may require additional confidentiality protection, translators may sign multiple NDAs depending on the project.

Job Competition System

When a job appears on the SayBon job board, a competition begins.

  • The first 3 translators to accept the job enter the editing phase
  • Once 3 translators accept, the job closes automatically
  • The job board then displays that the job has reached its acceptance limit

Quality Review

All submitted translations go through quality review.

  • Minimum pass mark: 85%
  • Submissions that fail review receive 0 payment
  • Rejected submissions receive an explanation

Payment Distribution

  • First translator to pass quality review receives 100% payment
  • Second translator to pass quality review receives 40% payment
  • Third translator to pass quality review receives 20% payment

Payment Timeline

Payments are issued within 3–5 working days after translation work successfully passes quality review.

Translator Verification

Applicants must provide identification and credentials.

  • National ID
  • Professional certification (if available)

Professional Translation Test

All applicants must complete a professional translation test.

  • The test must be completed inside the SayBon platform
  • The test cannot be copied
  • The test cannot be exported
