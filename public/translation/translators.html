SAYBON TRANSLATOR POOL

Collaborate with SayBon to deliver secure, professional document translations.
Information for Applicants ?

Language Requirements

  • Translators must be proficient in both French and English
  • Strong writing ability is required in both languages
  • Applicants should be experienced in translating professional documents

Professional Document Experience

Translators should be comfortable working with documents such as:
  • Legal documents
  • Corporate reports
  • Contracts
  • Academic papers
  • Technical documentation
  • Business communications

Work Is Performed Inside the SayBon Platform

All translation work must be completed inside the SayBon translation workspace.
  • Documents cannot be downloaded
  • Documents cannot be copied outside the platform
  • Documents cannot be stored locally
  • Documents cannot be transferred to external software
All translation activity must take place inside the SayBon platform to protect confidential client information.

Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA)

Before accessing any translation job, translators must sign a confidentiality agreement.
  • An NDA is required before job access
  • Multiple NDAs may be signed depending on job type
  • All documents handled through SayBon are confidential
Violation of confidentiality results in immediate removal from the translator pool.

Job Competition Model

When a new translation job appears on the job board, a competition begins.
  • The first three translators who accept the job secure access
  • Once three translators accept, the job closes to additional participants
  • The job board will display that the maximum number of translators has been reached
The competition then shifts to completion speed and quality.

Quality Evaluation

All submitted translations must pass quality review.
  • The minimum passing score is 85%
  • If a translation fails quality review, it will be rejected
  • A clear explanation will be provided for rejected work
Evaluation focuses on the quality and accuracy of the final translation.

Payment Distribution Model

Payment for a translation job is based on submission order and quality approval.
  • First translator to submit approved work receives 100% payment
  • Second translator to pass quality review receives 40% payment
  • Third translator to pass quality review receives 20% payment
If the first submission fails quality review, payment is not issued for that submission.

Payment Timeline

Payments are issued after translation work successfully passes quality review. Payouts are processed within 3�5 working days after approval.

Translator Registration Requirements

All applicants must complete a professional translation test during registration.
  • The test must be completed inside the SayBon platform
  • The test cannot be copied or exported
  • Applicants must provide national identification
  • Professional certifications may be requested
Identity verification is required before approval into the translator pool.

Work Availability

Joining the SayBon translator pool does not guarantee a fixed number of jobs. Work availability depends on:
  • Client demand
  • Language pair demand
  • Translator performance
  • Competition among translators
