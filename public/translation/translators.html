SAYBON TRANSLATOR POOL

Information for Applicants
Thank you for your interest in joining the SayBon Translator Pool. SayBon provides professional document translation services to individuals, businesses, and institutions. Our translators collaborate with us as independent professionals to deliver accurate, confidential, and timely translations. Before registering, please carefully review the information below.

Who Can Join

We welcome translators who:
  • Are fluent in at least two languages
  • Have strong writing ability in the target language
  • Have strong attention to detail
  • Can meet deadlines
  • Are comfortable translating professional documents
Examples of documents include:
  • Legal documents
  • Corporate reports
  • Academic papers
  • Contracts
  • Certificates
  • Technical documents

How the SayBon System Works

SayBon operates a centralized translation platform. Clients upload documents and request translation. The system processes the request and places the job into the translation queue. Translators from the pool may then view available jobs.
  • Translators work remotely
  • Translators choose when to accept jobs
  • Translators are independent collaborators

Competition Among Translators

The translator pool is competitive. Jobs may be assigned based on:
  • Speed of response
  • Translator rating
  • Previous performance
  • Specialization
  • Workload balance
Translators who consistently deliver excellent work receive more assignments.

Translation Quality Expectations

Translations must be:
  • Accurate
  • Natural in the target language
  • Grammatically correct
  • Faithful to the source meaning
  • Properly formatted

Confidentiality and NDA

All translators must sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement. Documents received through SayBon are confidential.
  • Documents must not be shared
  • Documents must not be stored permanently
  • Documents must not be reused
Violation of confidentiality results in immediate removal from the translator pool.

Important Security Rule — Work Is Done Inside the Platform

All translation work must be completed inside the SayBon translation workspace. Translators do not work in external software.
  • Documents cannot be downloaded
  • Documents cannot be copied outside the platform
  • Documents cannot be stored locally
  • Documents cannot be transferred to external tools
All translation activity must take place within the SayBon editing environment to protect client confidentiality.

Deadlines

Each translation job includes a deadline. Translators should only accept jobs they can complete on time. Missing deadlines affects translator eligibility.

Payment Structure

Translators are paid per word. Rates may vary based on:
  • Language pair
  • Document complexity
  • Urgency
  • Translator experience
Payments are calculated automatically after approval.

Work Availability

Joining the SayBon translator pool does not guarantee a fixed number of jobs. Work availability depends on:
  • Client demand
  • Language pair demand
  • Translator performance
  • Competition among translators

Before You Register

Before submitting your registration, ensure that:
  • You understand the workflow
  • You accept confidentiality requirements
  • You can meet deadlines
  • You are comfortable with the competitive translator pool
