SAYBON TRANSLATOR POOL

Collaborate with SayBon to deliver secure, professional document translations.
Information for Applicants ▼

Who Can Join

  • Fluent in at least two languages
  • Strong writing ability in the target language
  • Attention to detail
  • Ability to meet deadlines
  • Comfortable translating professional documents

How the System Works

Clients upload documents and request translations. The system generates a quote and creates a job. Translators from the pool can then accept available jobs. Translators are independent collaborators working remotely.

Translation Workflow

Client Upload → Quote → Payment → Job Created → Translation Queue → Translator Accepts → Translation Completed → Quality Review → Delivery

Important Security Rule

All translation work is performed inside the SayBon platform.
  • Documents cannot be downloaded
  • Documents cannot be copied outside the platform
  • Documents cannot be stored locally
  • Documents cannot be transferred to external software
This protects confidential client information.

Confidentiality

All translators must sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement. Documents must never be shared or reused. Violation results in removal from the translator pool.

Work Availability

Joining the translator pool does not guarantee a fixed number of jobs. Work depends on client demand and translator performance.
