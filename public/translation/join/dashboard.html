SayBon Translators

Translator Information

SayBon provides professional translation opportunities to a select network of verified translators.

Translators are assigned official client translation projects across business, legal, academic, and technical domains.

How assignments work

When a new translation project becomes available, it appears in your dashboard with full project details, compensation, and deadline.

The first three translators to accept the assignment secure participation in that project.

Once three translators accept, the assignment closes automatically.

Compensation structure

Each assignment displays the exact translator payment before acceptance.

The first translator whose submission meets SayBon quality standards receives the full payment.

The second and third qualified translators receive a partial performance bonus.

Payment eligibility depends entirely on quality approval.

Quality standards

All submitted translations undergo professional review and scoring.

Submissions must achieve a minimum quality score of 85% to qualify for payment.

Submissions that fail to meet quality standards are rejected.

Consistent performance ensures continued access to SayBon projects.

Confidentiality

All projects are confidential and must be completed exclusively within the SayBon platform.

Unauthorized copying, sharing, or external storage is strictly prohibited.

Access

Only approved translators receive secure access credentials.

If you are not yet registered, you must complete registration and verification.
