Prepare for the DELF Exam

A calm, structured space to prepare for one of the most important French language exams.
SayBon offers a guided preparation system designed to help you approach the DELF examination with clarity and confidence. Our preparation focuses on realistic exam tasks, structured practice, and clear progress tracking so that you know exactly where you stand before sitting the official exam.

What SayBon Offers

  • Preparation for DELF Prim, Junior and Tout Public
  • Support for levels A1, A2, B1 and B2
  • Listening, Reading, Writing and Speaking preparation
  • Mock-based learning from the start
  • Full exam-style mock tests
  • Preparation progress tracking and downloadable reports

Important to Know

SayBon is not an official DELF examination centre. We do not administer the official exam, issue official certificates, or register candidates for the exam. Our role is to prepare you so that you feel confident and ready when you take the official DELF exam.

How Preparation Works

1. Take a short level recommendation test. 2. The system suggests the DELF level that best matches your ability. 3. Register for preparation at that level. 4. Unlock structured mock-based preparation. 5. Track your readiness before taking the real exam.
