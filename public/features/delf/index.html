What SayBon DELF Preparation Offers

SayBon provides structured preparation designed specifically for the DELF examination. Instead of generic French lessons, the training focuses directly on the format, expectations, and evaluation style used in DELF exams.

• Realistic mock-exam style exercises from the beginning
• Familiarity with DELF listening, reading, writing, and speaking tasks
• Practice under exam-style conditions
• Preparation that focuses on exam readiness rather than general theory
• Progress tracking so you know when you're ready for the real exam

How Preparation Works

1. Take a short free level recommendation test.
2. The system determines which DELF level best matches your current ability.
3. Register for preparation at that level.
4. Begin structured mock-based preparation.
5. Track your readiness before sitting the official exam.

Preparation Fees

Payment is made once per level and gives you full access to everything that level offers.
There are no subscriptions or recurring fees.

Important to Know
